    Overview

    The Internet of things (IoT) brings together people, process, data, and things to make networked connections more relevant and valuable than ever before. IoT turns information into actions that create new capabilities, richer experiences, and unprecedented economic opportunity.

    The BTH-IOT-01-A can be powered by 48V as well as 5V via MicroUSB port. One of the most significant features is the BTH-IOT-01-A can measure a wide range of voltages from 1V to 24V, meeting industrial applications’ requirements for broad range voltage inputs. Other features include onboard environment sensor (Temp & humidity) to monitor the health of device

    The BTH-IOT-01-A is a compact, powerful control unit. It has several interfaces for fast and smooth integration in test environments. The BTH-IOT01-A has a wide operating temperature range, a robust design and high reliability.

    The BTH-IOT-01-A has various interfaces and ports SD card slot, MicroSIM etc, with support for GSM & WiFi. In addition it has hardware resources like Environment Sensor, A standby battery, RTC battery, for users to evaluate its performance and features

    It has a variety of input ports, including RS485, to attach different type of device/sensor. It also features 2 Dry contact outputs and 4 analog inputs.

    Its firmware is designed keeping all the industrial requirements in mind; to provide the easiest experience to the users while the BTH-IOT-01-A does all the heavy lifting. Every sensor/device attached continuously answers to the BTH-IOT-01-A, never letting a single bit of data going amiss: Only the polling string for the said device must be inserted once, during the configuration of BTH-IOT-01-A. Users can monitor the data from every sensor/device by simply logging into the server from anywhere, anytime

    Even if the BTH-IOT-01-A gets disconnected from the server, not a single moment of data is lost: the BTH-IOT-01-A is designed to keep the data logs stored till server is connected and then upload it all in sequence.

    Just by logging into the secure site address on the server, live data of device can be easily viewed by any number of users anywhere, who have been authorized by signing in.
