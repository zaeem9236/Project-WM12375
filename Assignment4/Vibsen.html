    Overview

    The VIBSEN is a highly effective form factor device that identifies and measures the occurrence of vibration at its point of installation. Configured to identify measures of vibration above a certain programmable threshold, the device generates an alarm in case of any intrusion. VIBSEN consume very little power to function and operates on DC power source

    With state-of-the-art Remote Monitoring capabilities, VIBSEN is equipped with advanced wireless functionality and faster uninterrupted communication, which surpasses standard vibration sensors susceptible to manipulation.

    VIBSEN is specifically designed to be deployed at remote sites to safeguard assets from theft, tampering or destruction. Batteries at hard to monitor telecommunication sites are often buried underground, walled off or kept in specially designed cabinets; VIBSENS design allows it to be used at fully functional capacity in all three use cases

    Multiple sensors encased in a waterproof body increase the longevity and environmental resistance of this solution. The system’s RS485 port allows for adjustable sensitivity as per the requirement of the asset VIBSEN is defending. The sensors, having a range of 200 meters, can wirelessly transmit threat alerts ensuring foolproof security of assets.
